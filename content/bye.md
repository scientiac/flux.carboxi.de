Goodbye originated by people saying **God Be With You** as a phatic, so often and rapidly that over time it mashed together to "godbewiye" and then finally to goodbye.

So, saying bye is like saying _Be With You_ very rapidly. Which makes saying bye to someone, very romantic to me.
